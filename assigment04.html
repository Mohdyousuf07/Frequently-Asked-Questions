FAQ

How much hours giraffe sleep ?

The average giraffe sleeps for 4.6 hours per day. View Source . For the most part, giraffes tend to sleep during the night, although they do get in some quick naps throughout the day. Giraffes can sleep standing up as well as lying down, and their sleep cycles are quite short, lasting 35 minutes or shorter.

Which animal sleeps for 2 hours a day ?

The *giraffe* is an animal that typically sleeps for about 2 hours a day. They often take short naps that last only a few minutes at a time, typically standing up to stay alert to predators.

Which anima sleeps 90% of its life ?

The *koala* is an animal that sleeps for up to 90% of its life. Koalas can sleep for 18 to 22 hours a day, largely due to their low-energy diet of eucalyptus leaves, which require long periods of rest to digest properly.
